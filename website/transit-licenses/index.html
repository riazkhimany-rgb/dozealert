Transit Data Licenses

Open data attributions for agencies supported in DozeAlert

Open data notice

DozeAlert uses public GTFS schedule and stop data from transit agencies. Data is cached on your device for offline alarms and is not redistributed by DozeAlert. Each agency sets its own open data terms — review them before downloading or importing feeds.

Bundled stop lists

GO Transit and TTC ship with bundled stop lists so alarms work before you download full GTFS. Bundled data may be older than the agency feed — download or import GTFS in the app for the latest stops.
