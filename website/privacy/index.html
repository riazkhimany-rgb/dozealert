Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 18, 2026 · App: DozeAlert (app.dozealert) · Developer: Riaz · support@dozealert.app

DozeAlert helps you wake up before you reach a selected destination using location-based alerts. This policy describes what information the app uses, where it is stored, and which third-party services are involved.

  • No account required — we do not operate a login system.
  • No advertising — we do not show ads or sell your data.
  • No DozeAlert cloud — trip data stays on your device.
  • Location is essential for trip monitoring and map features.
  • Google Maps Platform may receive data when you use map search or the map picker.

Information we collect

1. Precise location (GPS)

Latitude, longitude, speed, accuracy, and timestamps from your device while you use maps, pick a destination, or run trip monitoring (including in the background when monitoring is active). Used to measure distance to your destination and trigger alerts. Stored on your device only — not uploaded to DozeAlert servers.

2. Destinations and trip data you choose

Destination names, coordinates, wake radius, favorites, recent destinations, trip history (destination, times, alarm events), and monitoring settings. Stored on your device only.

3. App settings

Alarm volumes, transit agency preferences, theme, and onboarding progress. Stored on your device only.

4. Transit data (GTFS) you download

Public schedule/stop files downloaded from agency or open-data URLs you select. Cached on your device. Downloads use HTTPS to third-party hosts we do not operate.

5. Third-party services

Service When What may be sent
Google Maps Platform Map picker, place search Search text, map requests, location-related data per Google’s policy
GTFS / open data hosts Feed download Standard HTTP request metadata (URL, IP address)
Share targets you choose When you tap Share Only what you send via Android’s share sheet

We do not use analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting SDKs in the production app.

6. Support email

If you contact support@dozealert.app, we receive what you include in your message to respond to you.

How we use information

  • Monitor progress toward your selected destination
  • Deliver voice, vibration, and optional alarm alerts
  • Provide maps, place search, and transit features you enable
  • Save preferences and trip history on your device
  • Respond to support requests

We do not use your data for targeted advertising or selling personal information.

Retention and deletion

  • On-device data remains until you clear it or uninstall the app.
  • Clear your destination from the Home screen anytime.
  • Android Settings → Apps → DozeAlert → Storage → Clear storage deletes local data.
  • Uninstalling removes app data from your device.

We do not maintain a separate DozeAlert account database.

Security, children, and international use

Third-party API calls use HTTPS where supported. DozeAlert is not directed at children under 13. The app is developed in Canada; third-party processors such as Google may handle data under their own policies. Users in the EEA/UK may have additional rights — contact us at support@dozealert.app.

Google Play Data safety (summary)

  • Collected: Precise location; on-device trip/destination data; permissions as needed.
  • Shared: Location-related data with Google when using Maps/Places.
  • Not collected in-app: Account email, payments, ads profiles, health data.
  • Purpose: App functionality only.
  • Deletion: Uninstall or clear app storage.

Changes and contact

We may update this policy and change the “Last updated” date. The current version is always available here and inside the app (Settings → About → Privacy Policy).
