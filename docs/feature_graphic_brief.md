# DozeAlert Feature Graphic (1024 × 500)

Design instructions for the Google Play feature graphic.

## Canvas

- **Size:** 1024 × 500 px
- **Format:** PNG or JPEG, 24-bit, no transparency required

## Background

- Deep **midnight blue** base: `#0D1B2A`
- Subtle **diagonal gradient** to `#152536` (top-left to bottom-right)
- Optional soft **cyan glow** (`#4CC9F0` at 12–18% opacity) behind the icon, centered

## Hero Icon

- Large **sleeping face location pin** (same mark as app icon)
- **White** pin body, **cyan** closed eyes and smile
- Center-left or true center; keep important content within the central **safe zone** (avoid outer 10% edges where Play UI may crop)

## Typography

- **Primary title:** `DOZEALERT`
  - All caps, bold geometric sans (e.g. Inter, Google Sans, or similar)
  - Color: `#FFFFFF`
  - Size: dominant but not touching edges
- **Tagline:** `Never miss your stop again`
  - Sentence case, medium weight
  - Color: `#4CC9F0`
  - Placed below the title with comfortable spacing

## Style

- **Material 3** — rounded shapes, restrained shadows, high contrast
- **Premium & calm** — no clutter, no screenshots inside the graphic
- **No text** other than title and tagline
- **No Google Play badge** or device frames

## Export Checklist

- [ ] 1024 × 500 px exact
- [ ] Text readable at thumbnail size
- [ ] Icon crisp at 1x and 2x preview
- [ ] Brand colors match app (`#0D1B2A`, `#4CC9F0`, `#FFFFFF`)
