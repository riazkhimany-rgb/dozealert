DozeAlert helps commuters sleep peacefully and arrive confidently.

Whether you're travelling by train, bus, car, or rideshare, DozeAlert detects when you may be sleeping and wakes you before you miss your destination.

Features:

• Supports buses, trains and cars
• Smart sleep detection
• Loud alarm and vibration alerts
• Runs in background
• Adjustable sensitivity
• Battery optimized
• Simple and lightweight
• Ideal for daily commuters and long trips
