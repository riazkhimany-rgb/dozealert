DozeAlert helps commuters sleep peacefully and arrive confidently.

Whether you're travelling by train, bus, car, or rideshare, DozeAlert monitors your trip and wakes you before you miss your destination.

HOW IT WORKS
• Pick a destination on the map or search for a place
• Start monitoring — DozeAlert tracks your distance in the background
• Get a loud alarm and vibration when you're approaching your stop

FEATURES
• Bus, train, and car trips
• Smart sleep detection with adjustable sensitivity
• Background monitoring with a persistent trip notification
• Adjustable wake radius so you arrive on time
• Map destination picker and place search
• Saved favorites and trip history
• First-time setup guide with alarm test
• Transit schedules (GTFS) where supported, including Ontario and GTA agencies
• Battery-conscious design for daily commutes and long trips

PERMISSIONS
DozeAlert uses location (including in the background while monitoring), notifications, and optional battery optimization so alerts work when your screen is off. You control permissions in Android Settings.

No account required. No ads.
