# DozeAlert Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 2026

DozeAlert ("the app") is designed to help travelers wake up before reaching a destination. Your privacy matters to us.

## Location Data

- Location is used **only** to monitor your progress toward a selected destination.
- Location access is requested when you start monitoring or pick a destination on the map.
- We do not use location for advertising or unrelated tracking.

## Local Storage

- Favorite and recent destinations are stored **locally on your device**.
- Your selected destination and app preferences are saved using on-device storage.
- You can clear your destination at any time from the Home screen.

## Personal Information

- **No personal information is sold** to third parties.
- **No account is required** to use DozeAlert.
- **No tracking** analytics profile is created for advertising purposes.

## Future Cloud Features

Any future cloud backup, sync, or AI features will require **explicit consent** before your data is uploaded or processed off-device.
